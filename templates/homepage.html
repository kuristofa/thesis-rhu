About Us

Welcome to the Mexico Rural Health Unit, where health meets community.

Our mission is to provide comprehensive healthcare services to rural areas across Mexico, ensuring that every individual has access to quality medical care regardless of their location. Nestled within the heart of rural communities, we strive to bridge the gap between urban healthcare standards and the diverse needs of rural populations.

Our vision is to create healthier, happier, and more resilient rural communities by empowering individuals with the knowledge and resources they need to lead fulfilling lives.

Our Services

Primary Care

Comprehensive primary care services for individuals and families.

Health Education

Educating the community on health and wellness for a healthier lifestyle.

Preventive Care

Focus on preventive measures to maintain overall health and well-being.

Family Planning

Support individuals in achieving their desired reproductive goals while promoting the health and well-being of both the individual and the family unit.

Birthing Facility

Dedicated to provide exceptional care and support throughout the childbirth journey.

Disease Prevention and Control

Conducting screenings for diseases such as malaria, tuberculosis, and HIV/AIDS, along with implementing preventive measures and providing necessary treatments.
